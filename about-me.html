Payam Tabrizian

I am passionate about developing technologies and solutions that cross the boundaries between science and experience of a places - between how a space functions whitin an ecosystem and how people feel in that space. I develop methods for modelling and quantifying spatial attributes of landscapes from regional to micro scale, develop simulated and photorealistic immersive environments to visualize real-world experience of places, and design rigorous phsycological experiments to assess human's perceptual, cognitive and affective responses to their everyday envrionments. Other aspect of my work deals with tangible interfaces that allow experts and non-experts to intutively interact and make sense of complex geospatial information. I couple geospatial and 3D modelling/game engine software to generate on-the-fly realistic experience of otherwise abstract geospatial information. I am an open-science evangelist and commited to find open-source soloution.
